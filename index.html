About me

I am a recent grad student with major in Computer Science from The University of Texas at Arlington My under-graduate degree is also in Computer Science. I have a good understanding of Object Oriented Design and Design Patterns. My primary skills are in Java, SQL, JavaScript, HTML5, JSP and C++.

After almost 4+ years of academic and professional software development experience I can say these are the most important things I have learned:

  • Always try to build generic reusable modules.
  • Revisit your code. You will almost always find something to make it better.
  • Keep it simple.
  • Clean code is Good code. After all code is for humans!
